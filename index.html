Emotional Support Animal (ESA) Questionnaire

Please complete the clinical screening questions below. Answers are routed securely to your clinician.

About You

Your Mental Health

1. Have you ever been diagnosed with, or do you think you may be experiencing, a mental or emotional health condition? (For example: anxiety, depression, PTSD, ADHD, OCD, panic attacks, or similar.)

2. Does your mental or emotional health affect your ability to do everyday things — like working, sleeping, concentrating, taking care of yourself, or spending time with others?

3. How would you describe the current severity of your mental health symptoms?

4. Have you been experiencing these symptoms for 3 months or longer?

You & Animals

5. When you spend time around animals, do you notice a positive effect on how you feel — like feeling calmer, less anxious, or more grounded?

6. Do you have a specific animal in mind that you'd like to have as an Emotional Support Animal, or are you in the process of looking for one?

7. Can you describe, in your own mind, how having an Emotional Support Animal would specifically help with your symptoms or daily struggles?

Your Treatment & Support

8. Are you currently working with a mental health professional — such as a therapist, counselor, or psychiatrist?

9. Do you see an ESA as something that would work alongside your mental health treatment — rather than replacing it?

Housing & Purpose

10. Are you requesting an ESA letter primarily for housing purposes — for example, to live with your animal in a building that has a no-pets policy?

11. Were you aware that an ESA letter only provides protections in housing (under the Fair Housing Act) — and does not give your animal access to public places, restaurants, or flights?

12. Were you aware that online-only ESA certificates or "registrations" purchased on the internet are not legally valid in Florida?

Caring for an Animal

13. Do you feel you are in a stable enough situation — financially, physically, and in terms of your living situation — to care for an animal?

14. Are you aware that as an ESA owner you are financially responsible for any damage your animal causes to a property?

Social & Emotional Wellbeing

15. Do you often feel lonely, isolated, or like you don't have enough emotional support in your life?

16. Have animals — pets or otherwise — positively impacted your mental health or emotional state in the past or currently?

Additional Context

17. Do you currently have a routine or structure in your daily life, or is that something you find difficult to maintain?

18. Have you experienced trauma, grief, or a significant life stressor that continues to impact your emotional wellbeing?

19. How do you feel about your overall mental health right now, and your motivation to improve it?

20. Is there anything else you'd like your clinician to know about why you're seeking an Emotional Support Animal, or how you believe it would help you?
